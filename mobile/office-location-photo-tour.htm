Location Information

Hamilton Dental Group is conveniently located on the southwest corner of NW Washington Boulevard and Eaton Avenue on Hamilton OH's northwest side.

We are approximately two miles due west of SR 127 / South Riverside Drive, and 5.2 miles due east of SR 27, just south of our beloved Hamilton High School...GO BULLDOGS!

Immediately located to the west of our dental office is one of Hamilton's premier retail developments. It features big box retailers such as Wal-Mart, Lowe's, Rural King, Harbor Freight Tools, Kroger, and many others! Nearby local restaurants include McDonald's, Gina's Italian Kitchen & Tavern, Mad Mike's Burger & Fries, KFC, Texas Roadhouse, Kelly's Bakery, just to name a few.

Immediately east of our practice is the popular Fitton Family YMCA, a well known community center and workout facility for residents in Hamilton.

We proudly serve dental patients from all over northern suburbs of Cincinnati, including cities and towns such as Oxford, Middletown, Trenton, New Miami, Fairfield, Seven Mile, Pleasant Run Farm, Pleasant Run, Trenton, Forest Park, Mount Healthy Heights, Springdale, Beckett Ridge, Greenhills, and many many others.

We look forward to meeting you too!
