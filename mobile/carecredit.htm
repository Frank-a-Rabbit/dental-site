Maybe you'd like a brighter smile. Maybe you want to freshen your look. But if your insurance doesn't cover all the costs for the procedure you want and need, where do you turn? CareCredit, a GE Money Company, gives you convenient payment options so you can get the procedure you want, when you want it.

CareCredit gives you the freedom to get your procedure whenever you're ready. It's easy to apply. With three simple steps, including an instant approval process, you can say goodbye to the waiting time and reward yourself sooner.

Care Credit gives you a credit limit based upon your credit rating (regardless of how much you need for a service). There are no application or hidden fee's. They also have an estimated calculator on their home page so you have a good estimate of monthly costs when financing.

Applying for CareCredit is not only easy it's free and takes only five minutes.
