Meet Us

Peyman Chris Fattahi, DDS

Born in Italy and raised in a Doctors' Without Borders family he got to travel a lot and gain valuable life experiences in his youth. Dr. Fattahi is a highly trained dentist who graduated with honors from The Ohio State University. As a student there, he was awarded the American Association of Public Health Dental Student Recognition Award for his involvement in community outreach programs in SW Ohio. Prior to his DDS degree, he earned honors in Bachelor of biological sciences from Wright State University in Dayton, OH.

Dr. Fattahi brings his knowledge of the latest in dental technology to the practice, including ClearCorrect (clear aligners-Invisalign alternative), Clear Braces, and sleep apnea therapy.

Personal Life:
Dr. Fattahi is an enthusiast of the outdoors. He is an avid hiker, biker and kayaker.
He has a passion for animals, especially dogs. He enjoys spending time with his girlfriend, Kelly
and their two dogs Huey (Goldendoodle) and Paisley (Lab-whippet mix)!
